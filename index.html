FAQ

How many team members can I invite?

You can invite up to 5 friends to collaborate with you.

What is the maximum file upload size?

No more than 2GB. All files in your account must fit your allotted storage space.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click on "Forgot my Password". You will receive an email to reset your password.

Can I cancel my subscription?

We would hate to see you go, but you can cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard.

Do you provide additional support?

We're always available on our customer service hotline for you to reach out to us.
